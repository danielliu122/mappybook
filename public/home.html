Homepage

MappyBook is a Google Maps project that is designed to allow users to pin points to a map. Other users are able to see which points are on the map. In a business application, businesses could pin and advertise their businesses. Similarly, users could share locations they liked for others to visit.

Note: pins are currently stored in localstorage for demonstration

Functions:


  • Google Maps Search Bar
  • Long left click: create pin
  • Right click: hide pin
